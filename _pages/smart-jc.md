## Somatic MutAtion Research Trainee (SMART) Journal Club

Co-organized with <a href="https://nrockweiler.github.io/" target="_blank">Nicole Rockweiler</a>, <a href="https://www.ebi.ac.uk/research/coorens/" target="_blank">Tim Coorens</a>, and Ruitong Li, the SMART Journal Club has been active since April 2023, connecting 130+ Boston-area trainees in somatic mutation research. Our monthly journal clubs (lunch provided) feature discussions of high-impact new papers, led directly by the first authors. We also gather for happy hours (food and drinks provided) twice a year. 

Please check out [http://smartjc.broadinstitute.org/](http://smartjc.broadinstitute.org/) for more details. 

**Non-science note**: since I’ve been ordering food for our lunches and happy hours, here are a few I've found pretty good—lunch: Gifu Teriyaki & Sushi, Pho Viet, Flavor Boom!, OliToki, Felipe’s Taqueria, India Kitchen; catering: Tatte.
