## Somatic MutAtion Research Trainee (SMART) Journal Club

Co-organized with <a href="https://nrockweiler.github.io/" target="_blank">Nicole Rockweiler</a>, <a href="https://www.ebi.ac.uk/research/coorens/" target="_blank">Tim Coorens</a>, and Ruitong Li, the SMART Journal Club has been active since April 2023, connecting over 130 Boston-area trainees in somatic mutation research. Our monthly journal clubs feature discussions of high-impact new papers, led directly by the first authors. We also gather for happy hours twice a year.

Please check out [http://smartjc.broadinstitute.org/](http://smartjc.broadinstitute.org/) for more details.
